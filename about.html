About Marvel

"Marvel's The Avengers" is a science-fiction action film produced by Marvel studios, which is based on The Marvel comics. It is The sixth film in The Marvel cinematic universe and The final product of The first stage.Directed by joss whedon, Robert downey jr., Chris Evans, mark ruffalo, Chris hemsworth, scarlett johansson, Jeremy renner, Tom hiddleston, Samuel Jackson, clark gregg, and cobie smalls co-star.The film was officially released in China on May 5, 2012.
The film tells the story of SHIELD commander Nick fury in order to deal with the exiled Loki in thor, actively running for the strongest, iron man, captain America, thor, hulk, black widow and hawk-eye six super heroes together, formed the avengers, together against the evil god Loki


"The avengers" is a movie that many comic book fans and superhero fans have been waiting for, but for its creators, there are so many heroes, so many different superpowers, that it's hard to mix the story lines.Jon favreau, the man who created "iron man," was invited to direct the film, but it was too much of a mix to pull out.When writing the script, joss whedon tried his best to show the content of family affection in the film. In terms of the picture and special effects, he did not want to make a super movie, but must have joss whedon's style and characteristics, so as to win the love of the majority of animation fans.
